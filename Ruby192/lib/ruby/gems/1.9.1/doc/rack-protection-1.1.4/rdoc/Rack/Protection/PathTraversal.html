Rack::Protection::PathTraversal

Prevented attack

Directory traversal

Supported browsers

all

More infos

en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Directory_traversal

Unescapes ’/’ and ’.’, expands path_info. Thus GET /foo/%2e%2e%2fbar becomes GET /bar.
